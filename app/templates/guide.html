User Guide

Basic Navigation

  • New Flight: Record a new paragliding flight
  • Flight Records: View and manage all your flights
  • Statistics: Analyze your flight data

Recording Flights

When recording a new flight, you can input:

  • Flight title and date
  • Takeoff and landing details
  • Duration and distance
  • Altitude information
  • Weather conditions
  • Equipment used
  • GPS track and notes

Managing Flights

For each recorded flight, you can:

  • View: See detailed flight information
  • Edit: Update flight details
  • Delete: Remove flight records

Statistics Features

Analyze your flying progress with:

  • Total Statistics: View cumulative flight data
  • Total Avg. Statistics: View average cumulative flight data
  • Duration Trends: Track your flight duration progress
  • Altitude Analysis: Compare distance vs altitude
